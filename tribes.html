The Twelve Nations

  • New England: Maine, New Hampshire, Vermont, Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Connecticut
  • Greater New York City: New York City metro, Long Island, Northern & Southern New Jersey, Delaware, Maryland
  • Great Lakes: Western Pennsylvania, Ohio, Indiana, Illinois, Michigan, Wisconsin, Minnesota, Northern Missouri, Iowa
  • Appalachia: West Virginia, Eastern Kentucky, Eastern Tennessee, Western Virginia, Ozarks (Southern Missouri & Northern Arkansas)
  • Dixieland: Virginia (east), North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, Louisiana, Arkansas (south), Tennessee (west), Kentucky (west), Southern Missouri, Florida Panhandle, Northern Florida
  • South Florida: Everything south of the Orlando-Tampa line
  • Greater Texas: Texas, Western Oklahoma, Eastern New Mexico
  • The Great Plains: Nebraska, Kansas, Oklahoma (non-Texan parts), North Dakota, South Dakota, Eastern Montana, Eastern Wyoming, Idaho
  • Deseret: Utah, Nevada, Arizona, New Mexico (north), Western Colorado, Eastern California (desert regions)
  • California: Coastal & Central California (Bay Area, Sacramento Valley, Central Valley, Los Angeles Basin)
  • The Northwest: Oregon, Washington, Western Montana
  • Alaska: Entire state of Alaska
