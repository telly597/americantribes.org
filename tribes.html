The Twelve Nations

  • New England: Maine, New Hampshire, Vermont, Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Connecticut
  • Greater New York City: New York City metro, Long Island, New Jersey, Delaware, Maryland, Philadelphia
  • Great Lakes: Western Pennsylvania, Ohio, Indiana, Illinois, Michigan, Wisconsin, Minnesota, Northern Missouri (except Kansas City and St. Louis), Iowa
  • Appalachia: West Virginia, Eastern Kentucky, Eastern Tennessee, Western Virginia
  • Dixieland: Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, Louisiana, Arkansas, Tennessee (west), Kentucky (west), Southern Missouri, Florida Panhandle, Northern Florida, Kansas City, St. Louis
  • South Florida: Florida south of the Orlando-Tampa line
  • Greater Texas: Texas, Oklahoma
  • The Great Plains: Nebraska, Kansas, North Dakota, South Dakota, Eastern Montana, Eastern Wyoming, Idaho
  • Deseret: Utah, Nevada, Arizona, New Mexico, Western Colorado, Eastern California (desert regions)
  • California: Coastal and Central California (Bay Area, Sacramento Valley, Central Valley, Los Angeles Basin)
  • The Northwest: Oregon, Washington, Western Montana
  • Alaska: Alaska
